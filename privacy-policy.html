Section 04

Advertising

Pixel Dash: Endless Runner is free to play and may display advertisements, including optional rewarded ads that offer in-game benefits (e.g. extra lives, coin bonuses).

  • Personalised ads: Ad networks may use your Google Advertising ID (GAID) to serve ads relevant to your interests.
  • Non-personalised ads: You can opt out of personalised advertising at any time in your device's Google Settings → Ads → "Opt out of Ads Personalisation."
  • No forced ads: Rewarded ads are always optional. You are never forced to watch an ad to continue playing.
  • Children: If the device is identified as belonging to a child under 13, ad networks are configured to serve non-personalised, COPPA-compliant ads only.
⚙️

To reset or limit your Advertising ID on Android: Settings → Google → Ads → "Reset advertising ID" or "Delete advertising ID" (Android 12+).

Section 07

Children's Privacy (COPPA)

👦

Important notice for parents and guardians. Please read this section carefully.

Pixel Dash: Endless Runner is rated Everyone (E) on the Google Play Store and may be played by children. We take children's privacy seriously and comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and applicable international regulations.

  • We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.
  • The Game does not include social features, chat, or user-generated content.
  • Ad networks are configured to serve non-personalised ads when a child audience is detected.
  • No in-app purchases require interaction from children without parental consent.
  • If you believe your child has inadvertently provided personal information, please contact us at mlogesh2996@gmail.com and we will delete it promptly.
