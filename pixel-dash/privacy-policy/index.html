Short version: Pixel Dash: Endless Runner does not collect, store, or share any personal data. The game works fully offline and requires no account, login, or internet connection to play.

1. Overview

This Privacy Policy describes how LogeByte Games ("we", "us") handles information in relation to the mobile game Pixel Dash: Endless Runner (the "Game"), available on Google Play.

By downloading or playing the Game you agree to this policy.

2. Data We Collect

We collect no personal data whatsoever.

The Game does not use analytics, crash-reporting tools, advertising SDKs, or any third-party services. No information leaves your device. All game data (scores, progress, settings) is stored locally on your device only.

3. Internet Access & Permissions

The Game does not require an internet connection and does not request network permissions. It works entirely offline at all times.

The only device permissions the Game may request are those required for core gameplay (e.g. audio, vibration). No sensitive permissions such as camera, microphone, contacts, or location are used.

4. Children's Privacy

The Game is suitable for all ages. Because we collect no data at all, there is nothing to disclose, share, or delete regarding child users. We comply fully with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and Google Play's Families Policy.

5. Future Updates

We may introduce optional advertisements or analytics in a future version of the Game. If that happens, this Privacy Policy will be updated before the update is released, and you will be notified via the Google Play update notes.

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a revised effective date. Continued use of the Game after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the new policy.
